Cucumber Salad

Why Cucumber Salad?

So this Korean cucumber salad or side dish is great as a last minute kind of side dish since it takes less then 5 minutes to prepare. Well, depending on how fast you can cut! This cucumber salad or muchim can be a great side dish for Korean BBQ or for lunch. I actually got the idea to use this recipe becuase I always make it when I make Korean BBQ. Although, these cucumbers are honestly amazing on hamburgers, and sandwiches too. This recipe is actually my own I don't have a picture, hence the photo I borrowed from JinJoo at Kimchimari.com!
