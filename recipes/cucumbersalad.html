Cucumber Salad

Why Cucumber Salad?

So this Korean cucumber salad or side dish is great as a last minute kind of side dish since it takes less then 5 minutes to prepare. Well, depending on how fast you can cut! This cucumber salad or muchim can be a great side dish for Korean BBQ or for lunch. I actually got the idea to use this recipe becuase I always make it when I make Korean BBQ. Although, these cucumbers are honestly amazing on hamburgers, and sandwiches too. This recipe is actually my own I don't have a picture, hence the photo I borrowed from JinJoo at Kimchimari.com! But the rest of this recipe is straight from a broke college students kitchen (haha)!

How to Make Korean Cucumber Salad

Making this recipe is very, very easy. All you need to do is to make some cucumber slices, which I usually chop them about 1/4" inch thick. Then you just take a mixture of seasme oil, vinegar, salt, chili flakes, green onion, sugar, soy sauce, and roasted seasme seeds. Mix all the ingredients together, and then let the mixture sit in the fridge for at least 15 minutes, or overnight. It's really that simple!

Ingredients

  • 2 green onion,chopped
  • 1/4 tsp seasme seeds
  • 1/2 tsp Korean red chili falkes
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 cup tbps rice vinegar
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1 cucumber(Japanese, English,Pickling are all good) - about 1 1/2 cup sliced for 1 cucumber

Steps

  1. Slice cucumbers into thin slices. Around 1/4 inch
  2. In a sauce pan mix the soy sauce, vinegar, and sugar. Bring to a simmer, and then let cool down to room tempature.
  3. Then place the cucumbers in a sterilized jar, and pour the vinegar mixture over the cucumbers.
  4. Mix in the seasme seeds, Korean red chili flakes, and chopped green onions.
  5. Let the mixture sit in the fridge for at least 15 minutes, or overnight.
